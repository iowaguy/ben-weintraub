Ben Weintraub

About

I am a fourth year PhD student at Northeastern University in the Networks and Distributed Systems Security lab where I work with Cristina Nita-Rotaru. My research focuses on exploring security implications of the underlying network in distributed protocols. Before grad school, I was a Software Engineer in Silicon Valley.
